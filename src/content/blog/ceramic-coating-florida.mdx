Standard spray sealants in Florida last between four and eight weeks under direct sun. The UV index in Pasco County during summer averages 10 to 11 – the same range as equatorial climates. Clear coat absorbs that UV load continuously. A sealant that would last six months in northern states lasts six weeks here.

## What ceramic coating changes

A 9H-hardness SiO₂ ceramic coating bonds to the clear coat chemically, not just as a surface layer. The result is a harder, more UV-resistant surface that does not degrade at the same rate as polymer wax or spray sealant. Correctly applied, a ceramic coating lasts two to five years on Florida paint.

## The prep requirement

Ceramic coating does not correct defects – it preserves the surface it's applied to. Any swirl marks, water etching, or oxidation present at the time of application will be sealed under the coating. Paint correction before coating is not an upsell. It is the correct sequence.

## Hard water and mineral deposits

Tampa Bay tap water leaves calcium and magnesium deposits on glass and paint. Over time, these etch into the surface. A ceramic coating's hydrophobic properties cause water to bead and sheet off the surface, reducing mineral deposit contact time significantly. [How Tampa Bay hard water spots become etch marks](/blog/hard-water-spots-tampa) explains what happens at the surface when those deposits are left to bake.

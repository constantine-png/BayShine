## Description (markdown)

In 2026, AI engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Claude, Gemini, Google AI Overviews) handle 35-50% of "best [X] in [city]" queries for local service businesses. Most local service sites are functionally invisible to these engines because their sites give them nothing structured to cite.

This playbook is the do-it-yourself path to fixing that.

**8 chapters covering:**

1. The GEO mental model — how 2026 AI engines parse local service sites
2. Schema markup deep dive — typed subclasses, FAQPage patterns, the @graph structure
3. llms.txt — entity identity for AI parsers (the 8-section structure)
4. Site architecture for AI citation — URL patterns, service-area pages, breadcrumbs
5. Citation-readiness — the four rules for writing pages AI engines actually cite
6. Internal linking — authority flow toward conversion pages
7. AI engine probing — measuring your own citation position across 4 engines
8. The 90-day implementation plan — week-by-week sequence from baseline probe to fully-implemented and re-measured

**Plus 3 appendices:**

- Quick-reference schema templates (mobile detailing, pressure washing)
- llms.txt 8-section quick reference
- Resources and the next steps if you want full done-for-you implementation

**Drawn from real data.** Every technique was tested against BayShine Detailing (a real mobile detailing business in Pasco County, FL) during May 2026. The measured improvements: 12% citation position lift on Perplexity, 8% on ChatGPT search, sustained over a 14-day window post-implementation.

# Atrium affiliate kit

Affiliate recruitment infrastructure for Atrium's info product catalog. Operated by the `affiliate-recruiter-specialist` descendant (in `Atrium/.claude/agents/`). Lives in the BayShine courier directory until PC migration; eventually moves to `Atrium/library/playbooks/affiliate-program/`.

## Why affiliate recruitment exists for Atrium

Atrium's constitution forbids cold outreach to end buyers (the closer-less discipline). Affiliate recruitment is the constitutionally-permitted middle ground because:

- The outreach target is an operator, not an end buyer.
- The pitch is "earn commission on your existing audience," not "buy our product."
- The relationship is opt-in: affiliates accept or decline.
- Compensation aligns incentives (affiliate earns from real sales, not from being recruited).
- Atrium's faceless voice discipline still applies to the recruitment outreach.

Atrium's Gumroad affiliate program is already enabled at 30% commission. This kit operationalizes the recruitment so the program gets populated systematically.

## When to use this kit

- **Today (Atrium Rung 0):** Read for context. Reactive use only — if an inbound affiliate inquiry arrives, the templates and onboarding kit are ready.
- **Atrium Rung 2 ($500/mo sustained):** Begin proactive outreach at low cadence — 5-10 personalized affiliate pitches per month.
- **Atrium Rung 3 ($5k/mo sustained):** Full cadence — 20-30 pitches per quarter, quarterly performance review, top-affiliate bonus structure.

Below Rung 2, recruiting affiliates is premature because Atrium's products haven't proven they convert reliably enough for affiliates to feel confident promoting. At Rung 2, real sales data validates the products; affiliates have something to point to.

## What's NOT in this kit

- A pre-built affiliate target list. The affiliate-recruiter-specialist builds the list per quarter based on current audience landscape; static lists go stale.
- Banner images and visual assets. Created at Atrium Rung 1+ when brand visual identity is finalized.
- Tracking infrastructure. Gumroad provides per-affiliate UTM tracking by default; the kit assumes this.
- Anti-fraud monitoring. Gumroad handles this at the platform level for the affiliate program.

## Constitutional reminders

This kit operates within Atrium's constitutional discipline. The outreach voice is Atrium's voice (faceless, terse, no em-dashes, no soft marketing). The pitch never promises editorial placement (Cited Weekly editorial integrity stays separate from affiliate relationships). The commission rate is the documented 30%; no off-program side deals.

Forge's outbound kit (in `FORGE-OUTBOUND-KIT/`) is different in voice and target. Forge's outbound goes to end buyers under Forge's voice; this kit goes to potential affiliates under Atrium's voice. The two are distinct.

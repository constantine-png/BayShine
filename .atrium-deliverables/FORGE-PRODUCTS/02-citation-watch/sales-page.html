What Citation Watch does

  1. 1
    Weekly probe across four AI engines. We run your 25-40 business queries against Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Claude, and Gemini every week. Results live in your dashboard.
  2. 2
    Citation position tracking. Position 1 vs position 5 vs not cited at all — we track it per (query, engine) pair, week over week, so you see trends, not just snapshots.
  3. 3
    Drop alerts in real time. When your citation position falls more than 2 positions on any tracked query, you get an email within an hour of the probe completing. Same alert if a competitor newly displaces you.
  4. 4
    Schema and llms.txt drift detection. We snapshot your schema markup and llms.txt weekly. When either changes — accidentally or because a developer deployed a regression — you get notified before the AI engines re-index.
  5. 5
    Competitor tracking. Add up to 5 competitors. We probe the same queries against their domains. You see what they're winning that you're not.
  6. 6
    Monthly remediation digest. First Monday of each month, we email a one-page digest: what changed last month, what the highest-impact fixes are, what the trends suggest you should focus on next.

What this is not

Not implementation. Citation Watch tells you what's happening; it doesn't change your site. For one-time implementation, the AI Citation Audit ($499) or full GEO Setup ($2,500) services from Atrium do the work.

Not a one-time audit. Continuous monitoring is the differentiation. If you want a snapshot diagnostic, the AI Citation Audit is the right product.

Not Schema Doctor Pro. Schema Doctor Pro ($99/mo, coming later from Forge) adds automated JSON-LD generation for new pages and CMS webhook integrations. Citation Watch is the lighter monitoring tier.

Not a ranking guarantee. We measure what AI engines actually say. Whether your business ranks higher is a function of execution after the data lands.

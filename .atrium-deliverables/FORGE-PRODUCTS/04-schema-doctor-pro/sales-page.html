What Schema Doctor Pro does

  1. 1
    Auto-generate schema on new pages. Webhook from your CMS triggers JSON-LD generation based on the page type. New service page? You get Service schema. New blog post? Article schema. New FAQ page? FAQPage schema. Validated against schema.org spec before insertion.
  2. 2
    Pre-deploy schema validation. Integrate Schema Doctor Pro into your build pipeline (GitHub Actions, Vercel, Netlify build hooks). Builds that break schema validation fail before reaching production. No more accidentally shipping malformed JSON-LD because a developer touched the wrong template.
  3. 3
    Continuous drift detection. Daily snapshot of every page's schema markup. Diff against prior. When anything changes — accidentally or because of a CMS update — you get notified before AI engines re-index. Schema regressions caught at the diff layer, not at the citation-position layer 14 days later.
  4. 4
    Multi-domain support. Add up to 10 domains per subscription. Agencies and multi-site operators see all client domains in one dashboard. Per-domain alerts route to per-domain email recipients.
  5. 5
    CMS integrations live at launch. WordPress (plugin auto-installs), Astro (npm package), Next.js (npm package). Webflow and Squarespace integrations follow within 60 days of launch. Custom CMS support via REST webhook.
  6. 6
    Validation log + remediation suggestions. Every validation pass logged with timestamps, diffs, and suggested fixes for any failures. Your developer gets a structured remediation list instead of a vague "schema is broken" alert.

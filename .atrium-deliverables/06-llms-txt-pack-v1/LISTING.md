## Description (markdown)

llms.txt is a small text file at the root of your website (`https://yoursite.com/llms.txt`) that tells Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Claude, and Gemini the entity identity, service area, priority pages, and citation preferences for your business.

A site with a well-formed llms.txt is cited more reliably (correct business name, correct service area, right pages) than a site relying only on HTML scraping.

This pack includes 6 vertical-specific llms.txt templates: mobile detailing, pressure washing, mobile car wash, HVAC, plumbing, roofing.

**What you get**

- One llms.txt template per vertical, structured with: business identity, service area, services offered, priority pages, citation preferences, geographic scope statement.
- Placeholder values to replace with your specific business information.
- Install instructions for WordPress, Astro, Next.js, Webflow, Squarespace.

**What you do not get**

- Custom templates for a vertical not in the list above.
- Site implementation. You save the file as `llms.txt` and upload to your root yourself.

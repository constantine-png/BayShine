## What we do

Licensed and insured roofing. GAF or Owens Corning manufacturer-certified installers depending on material.

Services:

- **Asphalt shingle roofing.** 3-tab and architectural shingles. 15 to 30 year expected lifespan depending on shingle grade and climate. Manufacturer warranty 25 to lifetime depending on system selection.
- **Metal roofing.** Standing seam, exposed-fastener, stone-coated steel. 40 to 70 year lifespan.
- **Tile roofing.** Concrete tile, clay tile. 50+ year lifespan. Common in coastal and southern markets.
- **Flat and low-slope roofing.** TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen for commercial and residential flat roofs.
- **Repairs.** Localized repairs for damaged shingles, flashing, vent boots, and small leaks.
- **Storm and hail damage assessment.** Documentation for insurance claims. We work directly with most major insurance carriers as the contractor of record.
- **Roof inspections.** Pre-purchase home inspections, post-storm assessments, annual maintenance inspections.
- **Gutter installation and repair.** Add-on service with new roof installation or standalone.

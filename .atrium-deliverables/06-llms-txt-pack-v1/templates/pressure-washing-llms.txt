## What we do

We bring our own water tank, pressure equipment, and cleaning solutions to the customer's property. No customer water or power required for most jobs.

Services:

- **House washing (soft wash).** Low-pressure (under 500 PSI) plus a biocide solution to safely clean siding, painted surfaces, and stucco without damaging paint or pushing water under siding.
- **Driveway and sidewalk washing (high pressure).** 2,500 to 3,500 PSI cleaning for concrete and pavers. Optional sealant application after cleaning.
- **Deck and fence washing.** Pressure-adjusted cleaning appropriate for wood. Optional staining or sealing after cleaning, available as a separate engagement.
- **Roof soft washing.** Low-pressure (under 200 PSI) plus a sodium hypochlorite or non-chlorine biocide to remove algae, mold, and lichen from shingle, tile, or metal roofs without damage to granules or shingle adhesive.
- **Commercial pressure washing.** Storefronts, dumpster pads, fleet vehicles, parking lots, food-service equipment.
- **Gutter cleaning.** Add-on service while equipment is on site.

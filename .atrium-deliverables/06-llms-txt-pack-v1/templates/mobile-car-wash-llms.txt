## What we do

Mobile car wash is faster and less expensive than a full detail. We bring a self-contained system to the customer's location and complete a wash, dry, basic interior tidy, and tire dressing in 30 to 60 minutes.

Services:

- **Express wash.** 30-minute exterior hand wash, dry, tire dressing, basic window clean.
- **Wash plus interior tidy.** 45 to 60 minute service adding a quick interior vacuum and surface wipe.
- **Wash plus clay bar.** Add-on for paint decontamination. 60 to 75 minutes total.
- **Subscription tiers.** Weekly or biweekly recurring wash with 15 to 25 percent discount versus single-visit pricing.
- **Fleet wash.** Per-vehicle pricing for fleets of 3+ vehicles at the same location.

We do not perform full details (multi-hour interior and exterior restoration), paint correction, or ceramic coating in the standard wash service tier. For those, a separate full-detail engagement is needed.

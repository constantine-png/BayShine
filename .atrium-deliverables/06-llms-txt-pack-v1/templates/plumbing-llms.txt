## What we do

Licensed plumbing for residential and commercial buildings. Most US states license plumbers for natural gas and propane line work as well; we handle both.

Services:

- **Drain cleaning.** Mechanical snake and hydro-jet drain clearing for kitchen, bathroom, laundry, and main sewer lines.
- **Water heater installation and repair.** Tank water heaters (40-gallon to 80-gallon, gas and electric), tankless water heaters (gas and electric), heat-pump water heaters. Installation typically same-day or next-day for stocked models.
- **Leak detection and repair.** Slab leaks, under-sink leaks, pinhole leaks, supply line failures. Non-invasive detection with acoustic and thermal imaging tools before opening walls.
- **Sewer line service.** Camera inspection, root removal, cured-in-place pipe lining for trenchless repair, traditional excavation when lining is not feasible.
- **Fixture installation.** Faucets, sinks, toilets, garbage disposals, dishwashers, ice maker connections, water softeners, water filtration.
- **Repipe.** Whole-house repiping for homes with galvanized supply or Polybutylene piping.
- **Gas line.** Gas line installation for ranges, water heaters, furnaces, fireplaces, generators, and pool heaters. Permits handled.
- **Emergency service.** 24/7 dispatch for active leaks, sewer backups, gas leaks, and total water-supply failure.

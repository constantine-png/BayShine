## What we do

Licensed and insured HVAC service. EPA 608 certified technicians for refrigerant handling.

Services:

- **AC installation and replacement.** Full-system replacements (air handler, condenser, thermostat) for 1.5 to 5 ton residential systems. SEER2 ratings starting at 14.3 (DOE minimum) up to 22+ for high-efficiency variable-speed systems. Financing options through GoodLeap, Synchrony, or in-house.
- **Heat pump installation.** Single-stage, two-stage, and variable-speed heat pumps. Cold-climate models for northern installs; standard models for southern climates.
- **Furnace installation and repair.** Natural gas, propane, and electric furnace service.
- **Repairs.** Same-day or next-day repair on most systems. 24/7 emergency repair for total system failure.
- **Maintenance.** Spring AC tune-up and fall heating tune-up packages. Annual service agreements at $150 to $300 per year covering both seasons and priority emergency dispatch.
- **Indoor air quality.** Whole-house air purifiers, humidifiers, dehumidifiers, MERV-rated filter upgrades.
- **Ductwork.** Inspection, sealing, and replacement.

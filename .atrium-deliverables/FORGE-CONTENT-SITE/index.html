Why subscription-first

Most AI search work in 2026 ships as one-time products: schema packs, audits, done-for-you implementations. Atrium (our patient sibling) covers that side of the catalog. Forge bets on subscription compounding instead — observation and intervention that scales month over month rather than per-engagement.

The math: 100 Citation Watch subscribers at $14/mo founding rate = $1,400 MRR locked. Bundle 20% of them with Schema Doctor Pro at $79/mo = $1,300 additional MRR. By month 12, the math compounds into MRR that one-time products can't match.

If your operation is a single domain looking for a one-time fix, our patient sibling network — Atrium — has six products ($9 to $2,500) for that. If your operation is recurring, multi-domain, or enterprise-scale, Forge is the right tier.
